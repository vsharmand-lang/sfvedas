Three paths, built for three distinct types of Salesforce leader. Choose the one that matches where you are and where you need to go.

01

The Architect

For CTOs & Solution Architects

You need to understand Salesforce at the platform level — the architecture decisions that were made, why they were made, and what they mean for your enterprise. You're making decisions that will shape systems for a decade.

02

The Delivery Leader

For Delivery Managers

You're responsible for Salesforce implementations succeeding. You need to lead with confidence, anticipate architectural risks before they hit your project, and have intelligent conversations with your technical teams and your stakeholders.

Tutorials in this path

01 Salesforce Release Management — Building a Delivery Pipeline That Does Not Break Production → 02 Building a Salesforce Centre of Excellence — Why Most Fail and What the Successful Ones Do Differently → 03 Is Salesforce Truly Agile-Ready, or Are We Forcing Agile onto a Waterfall Platform? → 04 Salesforce Technical Debt — The Invisible Risk That Destroys Delivery Velocity →
03

The Strategist

For All Senior Tech Leaders

You're making the big decisions — invest, build, buy, or walk away. You need a framework for evaluating Salesforce as a strategic platform, not just as a product, and the confidence to challenge the received wisdom in the room.
