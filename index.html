The gap we fill

Most Salesforce content
teaches you what to click.
This teaches you what to think.

Trailhead is excellent for beginners. Certification content is excellent for passing exams. But there is a layer above that — the architecture tradeoffs, the delivery situations that go wrong, the commercial questions nobody answers honestly — where almost nothing has been written down properly.

SFVedas exists for that layer. Written for architects, delivery managers, and tech leaders who are already fluent in Salesforce and need to go deeper.
